About Bright & Right Line Painting

Bright & Right Line Painting is one of Ottawa’s most experienced line painting companies. We are equipped for large and small jobs and guarantee quality workmanship. With over 30 years of industry experience, we provide reliable and speedy traffic signage, asphalt repair, and pavement markings for clients. We offer competitive quotes and strive to complete all jobs promptly and efficiently. Although commercial line painting is our specialty, we’re equally renowned for our accurate assessments, quick service, and results that truly make a difference. We provide free estimates and are available 24/7 for your convenience and peace of mind.
